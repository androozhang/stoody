Get Help!

Timer

To use the timer function:
Click on the clock icon and a timer will appear! There is a section for "studying" and a "break". Set your desired amount of time and when the timer reaches the end, a sound will go off.

Tasks

To use the task function:
Click on the task button and then a blank list will appear. On the top of that list, write the tasks you would like to complete, then click "add". When finished with that task, you can cross it off by clicking on the preferred task.

Flashcards

Need to study using flashcards?
Click on the flashcard icon and it will take you to Stoody's flashcard feature. Here, you can add flashcards and use them to study! 

Backgrounds

Want to change the backgrounds?
By clicking on the picture icon, you can change the backgrounds of the website. Through our wonderful list of videos we currently have, you can choose one of your liking! 

Music

Need some background noise?
You can play music from our website! Click on the music icon and there will be options of audios to choose from. Adjust the volume to your liking and play/pause the music as well.

Tips & Tricks

How to implement stoody into your studying routine!

Backgrounds

Change the background to your liking! Whether it's a cafe, sea animals or seeing another person study, use it to your advantage to elevate your environment.

Music

Don't want to listen to music while studying? Look at stoody's option's for audio's to play in the background! Lofi, Minion music, or rain!

Pomodoro Timer

Use the pomodoro timer to split your study session into parts! Study for 25 minutes, then give yourself a 5 minute break! This will maximize your study-break time's to the fullest!

Extra Studying Advice:

Put your phone away!

Let's be real, your phone is one of the top reasons why you don't get much work done! Let's put the distraction source away so you can get your work done as fast as you can! Put your phone on do not disturb or lock in a different room. Anything to make sure that it does not bother you while you are studying!

Drink Water!

Your body needs water! Make sure you drink water before, during 
and after you study! Have a nice glass beside you and be sure 
to allow yourself to be hydrated!

Have a snack!

Your brain feeds off of sugar! Allow yourself to have a piece of candy 
(not too much) to jump start your studying session! Have a small 
plate of some sugary candies, or consume one during your break session!

Sleep!

This cannot be emphasized enough! Sleep is essential for your body to 
recharge and get the brain power needed to study. Allow yourself to sleep at least 6-8 hours. This sleep will give you the much needed rest that you deserve.

Stretch!

Sitting for hours on end puts a toll on your body. During your break times, 
get up and stretch! Do some jumping jacks and walk around a little bit. 
By moving your body around, you can give yourself a break from long 
study sessions and move around
